Match the correct beat sequence to beat the break

How To Play
GAME
OBJECTIVE

Learn how drum beats are programmed into a drum sequencer.
Match the correct beat sequence for each of the four drums: Kick drum, snare drum hi-hat cymbal and open hi-hat cymbal. Pass all 3 levels to win.

GAME
DIFFICULTY

Easy Mode:
20 Shots • Slow Tempo

Normal Mode:
10 Shots • Medium Tempo

Hard Mode:
5 Shots • Fast Tempo

— GAME PLAY —

  • Click "PLAY" on the drum console to start the sequencer.

  • Watch the light blink through each pad and click the pad that plays the the pad will then turn green and and will program the drum sound to play.

  • Select all the correct pads to move to the next drum.

  • Complete each drum pattern to advance to the next level.
    Reach level 3 and you win!

  • Select the wrong pad and lose a shot. Lose all shots and it's game over.
